Reporting Issues

If you have any issues with the Hack Clock, or find an error in the documentation, or just need something clarified - feel free to send me a message on my Hackaday Project message board - I'll respond as soon as I see it.

If you found a bug or identified a place in the Hack Clock libraries that doesn't quite work, go ahead and open a case on the Hack Clock Issues Board, along with steps to reproduce the bug. I'll keep that issue up-to-date and let you know when it closes.

Thanks for letting me know what you find!
